Brand Mission: Our mission is to provide informative and actionable content to help small businesses succeed with social media. We value clarity, simplicity, and a friendly, encouraging tone.

Brand Voice:
- **Tone:** Expert, but approachable.
- **Style:** Use short, punchy sentences.
- **Vocabulary:** Avoid technical jargon. If it must be used, provide a simple explanation.
- **Engagement:** End every blog post with a clear, open-ended question to encourage comments and community interaction.

Core Values: We believe in transparency, authenticity, and empowering our readers.
