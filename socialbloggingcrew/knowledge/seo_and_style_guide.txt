Content Policy:
- All blog posts must maintain a positive and professional tone. Avoid controversial or political topics.
- All content must be original and free of plagiarism.
- Headlines must use title case (e.g., "The Top 5 Tips for Social Media Marketing").

SEO Guidelines:
- Primary keyword must be in the title and the first paragraph.
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags in a separate section at the end of the post.
- Use a clear hierarchy of headings (H1, H2, H3) for readability.
- Meta-descriptions should be 1-2 sentences and include the primary keyword.
